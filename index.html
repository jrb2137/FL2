Product Description

Are you ready to be the most memorable teacher of a child's life? Use these step by step instructions to guarantee student engagement while they learn about numbers 1-10 to solve classroom mysteries. With very little prep, your students will work as a team to break into a box, which breaks them out of the classroom as they beat the clock! #hotsummerdeals

What's inside this download?

There is aPDF file with step-by-step instructions, a link to incorporate a YouTube Video to HOOK your students into the mystery about cute, but naughty TROLLS capturing their classroom and pre-made team names with mysteries to deal with finding missing numbers in a line up, color coding a mystery picture and using a magnifying glass to find little numbers on a key.
